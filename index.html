Deerfield Left Alliance

A left-aligned progressive movement determined to educate others on the need for systemic change in the current system.

  • Chevron Down (Light)

Our beliefs

When joining the DLA, it is important to keep these core values at heart.

Societal issues and economic problems are relevant and persistent. Inequality by class, race, gender or sexuality remains a longstanding issue looked over by many Americans today. There is a lot of injustice and poverty in our own society and with constitutional and legal change stagnant we must bear the burden and attempt to correct the many issues of previous generations. While true equality for all is impossible at this point in time (or at least through democratic means it is) that does not mean that we cannot fight for progress in America through our own acts of activism.

What we will do

Discuss and act on topics relevant to today.

Discuss current events, politics and the general social and economic positioning of our world today. Engage in Left-aligned Literature by reading books by leaders of equality (e.g. Autobiography of Malcolm X or Crusade for Justice by Ida Wells). Attend protests and rallies that support our causes. Work with different charities, especially ones focused on change and equality to achieve our goals of activism for the benefit of society. Educate and take action, while defeating misrepresentation in the media and in our own school curriculum.

Composition

Anyone left-aligned can join.

DLA in High School would encompass a large variety of left-aligned views from democratic socialists to orthodox Marxists. We believe there are many who share progressive values and would love to be a part of making our world a better place.

Joining the DLA

To join, all that has to be done is joining the Discord. If you don't have Discord, you can still participate by emailing us.
